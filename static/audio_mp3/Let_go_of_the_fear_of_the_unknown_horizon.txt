"Let go of the fear of the unknown horizon,  and immerse yourself in the present's vivid canvas,  for therein lies true living. " 
This wisdom invites us to release the burdensome fear of the future and focus on the present. 
By fully engaging with the tapestry of life before us,  we can appreciate its richness and meaning. 
Embrace the opportunities that each moment offers,  for it is within the present that life unfolds,  and it is where we can shape our own destiny.
