"Live your life as if it were to recur eternally,  for in that reflection,  you'll find purpose and meaning in each fleeting moment. "
This thought-provoking quote draws attention to the significance of our actions in the grand scheme of existence. 
If we envision our lives repeating infinitely,  we are challenged to make each choice deliberately and find profound purpose in the present
By living with this eternal recurrence in mind,  we gain a deeper understanding of the value of time and the significance of our endeavors.
